First step
  • First, we must cut the tamarinds into pieces and remove the seeds. In a pot, place the tamarind, the sugar, the lemon, orange and, if you like, the cinnamon stick.

    The ingredients should be placed on a medium heat, until it starts to boil. It is necessary to stir constantly, always with a wooden spoon. It usually takes an hour to cook.
    • Second step
  • We let cool and begin to store it in glass containers. It should be refrigerated and can be consumed with toast or toast.
    • Third step
  • If it's your first time making a jam, and you do not know its texture when it's ready; you should notice that it is thick and when grabbing it with the wooden spoon, a large part of the mixture remains in it.
    • Fourth step
  • There are people who like to put the seeds. If you want to place it, a recommendation is that you do it when you are storing it, never cook your tamarind jam with the seeds.
    • Fifth step
  • In some occasions, the seeds can bring an acidity, therefore it will take away flavor from your jam and nobody will want to consume it. That is why it is not recommended to cook with the seed, if you do not want to place the orange zest you can remove it, generally these grated rind does not reduce flavor, instead it intensifies it.
    • Sixth step
  • Having already the ingredients and the steps for the preparation, encourage yourself to prepare it. Take a risk to always add something new, you could change or substitute some ingredient, to achieve a good structure and flavor. Use your creativity a little, so that your friends and family are left wanting to continue tasting your jams. Not everything goes well the first time, so be patient, and as you continue to do it, you will be able to polish all those mistakes that you do not like, and you will get the flavor you are looking for.
