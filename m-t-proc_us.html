First step
  • The first thing to do before preparing the tomato jam is to peel and chop all the tomatoes. If it costs you a little to peel the tomatoes, we recommend that you make a cross at the base, put water to boil and heat them for a few seconds. Surely so you will not have problems to remove the skin.
    • Second step
  • Once peeled and chopped, weigh the tomatoes to balance the amount of sugar. To make the tomato jam you will need to calculate the sugar based on the weight of the tomatoes. You must use half the weight of tomatoes in sugar. So, if they are now 450 g of tomatoes, you will need 225 g of sugar.
    • Third step
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, sugar and lemon juice to a saucepan and let it stand for 2 hours or 3. In this way all the ingredients will be mixed well and it will be easier to dissolve them in the fire. With this jam you can make different and original tomato desserts, or prepare snacks with toast using a different one to the fruit jam that you normally use.
    • Fourth step
  • Over time, put the casserole to heat over low heat and stir constantly the mixture with a wooden spoon, or other kitchen utensil, until it becomes a consistent homogenous mass. Check its thickness by dropping some drops, if they are liquid it still needs more time, if on the other hand they fall slowly and are grouped means that the tomato jam is ready. Approximately 45 minutes will be required.
    • Fifth step
  • Remove the jam from the fire a few minutes before reaching the proper thickness as it will still become more compact and consistent when cooled. Store the tomato jam in jars with airtight seal so that it does not spoil and keep until the moment of taking. As you can see, making tomato jam is very simple and does not require hard-to-find or expensive ingredients. Put this recipe to the test and prepare desserts with tomato, snacks or different breakfasts.
