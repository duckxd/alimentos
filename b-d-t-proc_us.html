First step
  • PELA the tamarind and rinse it with bottled water or filtered.
    • Second step
  • ADD clean pulp and add water until it covers, bring to a boil and allow it to boil for 10 minutes or until it softens.
    • Third step
  • SET and remove the remaining bones and fiber.
    • Fourth step
  • PLACE the pulp in a saucepan, pour the ½ cup of water and sugar; Heats fire and keeps moving. Let it thicken, the tamarind will be ready when you pass the spoon and you can see the bottom. Remove and let cool.
    • Fifth step
  • FORM balls with the pulp and reserve them. You can cover each ball with chili pepper and / or refined sugar, even with glass sugar. Place the bags in canastitas or fill them with cellophane.
