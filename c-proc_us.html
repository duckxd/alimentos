First step
  • The first thing is to preheat the oven to about 190 º C. The dough is very easy and fast and as we have everything mixed goes directly to the oven.
    • Second step
  • We separate the whites from the yolks, we reserve.
    • Third step
  • Add the egg whites and the egg yolk together with the sugar and salt in a large bowl and beat well until you get a frothy and fluffy mixture. Then add the previously sieved flour and beat everything again.
    • Fourth step
  • Finally we add the coconut, we mix everything very well with a fork and we already have the mass of the cocaditas, it must be a creamy and homogeneous mixture. Try it, even raw is delicious.
    • Fifth step
  • Prepare the oven tray with vegetable paper or a silicone mat.
    • Sixth step
  • With a spoon, since the dough is quite creamy, we are making small portions or balls the size of a walnut.
    • Sixth step
  • We deposit them in the tray (with enough space so that when they are made they do not stick to each other, about 3 cm is enough). On my baking tray about 12 cocadas per batch, if you want more you just have to multiply the quantities.
    • Sixth step
  • Bake for 15 minutes at 180 º C in the middle tray. With heat, air up and down, until they are golden brown.

    As I said everything depends on the oven of each one, in some it will be 14 minutes in another 20.

    It looks perfectly when they are ready because they are taking a nice golden color. The only care you should have is that you do not get burned.

    When they are golden they will be dried and toasted on the outside and creamy on the inside.

    We take them out of the oven to harden and cool. It is important not to touch them when they are still hot since they may be soft.

    Once they are cold, they are removed from the oven tray and ready to serve.

    We store them in an airtight container so that they do not soften and are of vice, to repeat. Be careful because they are simply addictive, highly addictive.
