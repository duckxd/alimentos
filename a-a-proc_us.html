First step
  • The one large satin, toast the amaranth over medium heat, for about 10 minutes or until it turns golden. Menéalo constantly so that this does not burn and the gold is even.

    Once it is ready let it cool in a large bowl.
    • Second step
  • In the same skillet toast the seeds (pumpkin seeds) until they are golden brown and repeat the procedure for the nuts.

    The three ingredients will brown in the same way but try to do it separately because each of them needs a different time.
    • Third step
  • Once you have finished browning, add the amaranth, nuts, pips and raisins in a single bowl.
    • Fourth step
  • Break the piloncillo into small pieces and place it in a large saucepan along with the honey and water.
    • Fifth step
  • Cook over low heat, constantly moving. until you get a syrup without lumps. Once you have it, remove it from the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes.
    • Sixth step
  • Mix the amaranth preparation with the syrup, trying to make everything come together in a single mixture.
    • Sixth step
  • Line a cookie sheet with wax paper and pour the mixture, use your hands to spread and flatten the amaranth.
    • Sixth step
  • Let rest for an hour and dismantle.

    Use a wet knife to cut the candy into the pieces you like.
