First step
  • Preheat the oven to 200ºC.

    Unroll the puff pastry and mark with a knife around the dough 2 cm from the edge. Prick the dough minus the edge.
    • Second step
  • Wash the apples and without peeling cut into thin slices.
    • Third step
  • Place on top of the puff pastry in a circular shape.
    • Fourth step
  • Divide the butter into pieces and sprinkle with the sugar.
    • Fifth step
  • Bake for about 20 minutes until golden brown.
    • Sixth step
  • Remove from the oven and brush with the warmed marmalade a little in the microwave (if it is very thick, add a little water).
