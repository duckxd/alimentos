First step
  • Preheat the oven to 375˚F.
    • Second step
  • Preheat a skillet over medium heat for 1 minute. Remove the chorizo ​​wrap and cook 1-2 minutes. Tearing it with a spoon while cooking. Add the onion, and continue cooking 3-4 minutes. Remove from the stove and set aside.
    • Third step
  • In a large bowl, combine ground beef with egg, bread crumbs, Cotija cheese, salt, and pepper. Mix well until everything is combined
    • Fourth step
  • Cover a medium tray with waxed paper. Place the meat and flatten it evenly. Remove the meat from the tray with everything and the wax paper to a flat surface. Add 1 cup of Chihuahua® Cheese on top of the meat, then a layer of chorizo, spinach, Add the rest of the cheese. Carefully roll the meat until it forms a roll.
    • Fifth step
  • On a large piece of wax paper, place the 10 slices of bacon from side to side. Place the meat on top and roll with the bacon. Carefully place the meat roll with the bacon opening down in a (9 "x 13") ovenproof pan, Bake for 45 minutes or until the center of the meat reaches a temperature of 160 ° F. Increases the temperature of the stove to grill (broil). Bake for 4-5 more minutes, or until the bacon is golden and crispy.
    • Sixth step
  • Let stand 10-15 minutes before cutting. Serve and enjoy!
