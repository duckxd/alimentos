First step
  • Wash the red plums very well because you will cook them with skin. Remember when you buy the plums try to be ripe but not over, not very green.

    Tip: The amounts of this recipe yields for 2 small pots of homemade red plum jam.
    • Second step
  • Cut the plum a little before half, remove the bone. Chop the fruit in 4 parts and reserve.
    • Third step
  • Place the plum pieces in a large pot with 1/2 cup of water. Cook over medium heat for 10 minutes. Remove until the fruit is disintegrated.
    • Fourth step
  • After the time, let cool the preparation at room temperature. Then, grind with the food processor of your choice.

    Tip: You can skip this step if you prefer a homemade red plum jam with more pieces of fruit.
    • Fifth step
  • Pour the crushed fruit in a casserole dish. Add the sugar and stir until it is completely integrated. Cook for 15 minutes over medium heat.
    • Sixth step
  • Finished the time, add the spices and the juice of half a lemon. Wait 5 more minutes before removing the casserole from the heat. Take out the spices and pour the jam into jars.

    Tip: Remember that after cooling the jam acquires more consistency.
    • Seventh step
  • This red plum jam step by step is simply ... delicious! The acid-sweet contrast between plums and sugar, plus the exotic aromas of the species, make it a gourmet preparation.

    Tip: The total cooking time of this marmalade is 30 minutes.
