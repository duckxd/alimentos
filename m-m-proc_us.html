First step
  • First, peel and chop the mango. Then we weigh the pulp without skin or bones. My mango weighed 500 g so I will use 250 g of sugar, since you have to use half the amount of sugar as of fruit.
    • Second step
  • Then we wash and squeeze a lemon.
    • Third step
  • We introduce the diced mango, 250 g of sugar and the juice of a lemon in a casserole dish. We stir the mixture a bit and cook it over medium heat for about 20 minutes. You have to remove the jam from time to time to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pan. After 20 minutes, the fruit will have been quite tender, so we can put out the fire.
    • Fourth step
  • Once we have removed the jam from the fire, we must pass the blender to achieve a homogeneous texture.
    • Fifth step
  • Finally, you have to boil a couple of glass jars in a pot to sterilize them and pour the jam inside. We must be very careful not to burn ourselves. Then close the jars well and put them face down until they cool.
    • Sixth step
  • Now you can store this delicious homemade mango jam in a cool, dry place away from sunlight. This will keep it in good condition for about six months. Remember that, once opened, you have to store the jam in the refrigerator or refrigerator to keep it properly.
