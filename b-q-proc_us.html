First step
  • Boil the potatoes; Once boiled remove from heat and strain. Step on the potatoes adding the butter, pepper and salt to taste.

    Add the egg (or both yolks) to the puree and mix well. Add the flour little by little and mix until it forms a more consistent mash and does not stick on the fingers.
    • Second step
  • Assemble the pumps with the mashed potatoes and the mozzarella cheese.

    Take a piece of puree, crush it well on the palm giving it the shape of a disk (go assembling the bombs one by one).
    • Third step
  • Place some mozzarella cheese in the center of the mashed disc.

    Close the disc so that the cheese is inside.
    • Fourth step
  • Give round shape to the pump, taking care that the cheese does not come out, this must be completely covered by the mash.
    • Fifth step
  • Pass the pump through flour, then through a beaten egg and bread crumbs.
    • Sixth step
  • To fry in abundant hot oil until they are golden, but taking care of not leaving them much longer so that they do not open.

    Remove the oil pumps with a slotted spoon and place them in a tray with absorbent paper.
